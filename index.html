About

Shmears Draft League is a competitive 12-team league divided into four divisions. Over an 11-week season, each team battles every other team once. The league is run by @elziee, @shmear, @neto9, and @for.the.faithful. Live scores, standings, player movement, and transactions are tracked here all season long.
